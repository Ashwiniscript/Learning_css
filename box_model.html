A paragraph is a distinct unit of writing, typically composed of multiple sentences, that focuses on a single idea or topic. It serves to organize and structure written work, making it easier for readers to follow the author's train of thought. Paragraphs are fundamental to longer pieces of writing, such as essays, papers, or even emails, helping to break down complex information into manageable chunks.
